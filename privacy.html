Advertising and Personalized Ads

Google AdMob Integration

Our applications utilize Google AdMob to display advertisements. This integration enables us to offer our apps for free while maintaining continuous development and improvement.

How Advertising Works in Our Apps

We partner with Google AdMob to deliver advertisements within our applications. To provide you with a more relevant and engaging advertising experience, certain data may be collected and processed:

  • Device Identifiers: Including advertising IDs that help deliver and measure ad performance.
  • Usage Patterns: How you interact with our apps, including session duration and feature engagement.
  • General Location: Approximate geographic location based on IP address (not precise GPS location).
  • App Interaction Data: Information about which ads you view and interact with.

Personalized Advertising

By default, Google may use the collected data to show you personalized advertisements that are more relevant to your interests. This personalization is based on:

  • Your activity within our apps and other apps using Google's advertising services
  • Information associated with your Google account (if applicable)
  • Your general interests and demographics inferred from your online activity

Your Choice Matters: You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy > Advertising. On Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads. You may also adjust your ad preferences in your Google Account settings.

Third-Party Advertising Partners

Google AdMob may work with various advertising partners to display ads. These partners may collect and use data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy for comprehensive information about how advertising data is handled.
