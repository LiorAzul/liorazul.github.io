Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the login screen and tapping "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Alternatively, you can email us at Liorazulay72@gmail.com and we'll process your request within 48 hours.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Force close the app and reopen it. 2) Make sure you have the latest version installed. 3) Restart your device. If the issue persists, please contact us with details about when the crash occurs.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account you used for the original purchase.
